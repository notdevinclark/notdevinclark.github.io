About Me

Hello there, my name is Devin Clark and I am Senior Software Engineer that has a passion for learning new technologies and exploring efficient solutions to complex problems.

I have been writing software professionally for the last 12 years, primarily working with Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Throughout my career I have done frontend work with Javascript, React, and CSS with backend work using Ruby, Java, Kafka, GraphQL with a smattering of different databases like MySql and Postgres. In my spare time I have been diving more into Java as well as Elixir, Python, and Machine Learning.

I love software development’s ever changing landscape of new ideas and approaches to solving different problems. I never want to be held back by the phrase “We have always done it this way!".

Outside of tech, you will find me spending time with my corgi Maple, out on a trail or if I am lucky, walking on the beach. And when the weather does not allow that, you can find me hosting a game of Dungeons & Dragons as a DM or playing video games.

If you share my passion for well written, efficient software and like to think outside the box, I would love to connect. Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn.
